✨ About Me

Hey, I'm Aarushi 👋

I'm a beauty, lifestyle & fashion content creator based in India — turning everyday moments into content that makes you actually stop scrolling. From skincare routines to outfit inspo, I share what I genuinely love, just with extra drama, detail, and personality ✨

With 14.3K followers and 800K+ monthly reach, I create for brands with one goal: content that doesn't feel like an ad but still makes people want the product. Strong hooks, fun concepts, clean edits — and I'm always tapped into what's working (and what's overdone).

Off-camera? Probably café-hopping, obsessing over new launches, or planning my next outfit like it's a full production 🎬
